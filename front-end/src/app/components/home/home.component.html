MONITORING

Monitoring and logging play a crucial role in DevOps by providing real-time insights into system performance, identifying issues, and ensuring efficient operations. Tools like Prometheus offer powerful metrics collection and alerting capabilities, while Grafana provides flexible visualization of data. The ELK stack (Elasticsearch, Logstash, and Kibana) is widely used for log management, analysis, and monitoring. Together, these tools empower teams to proactively address performance bottlenecks and maintain system reliability.
